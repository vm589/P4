Brass instruments have been around for as long as ancient history has started to be recorded. It is a very important part of human culture as a whole from ancient egypt to medieval period, and even today it is used. The earliest documention of brass instruments can be dated back to 1500 B.C. The earliest brass instruments used to be made of copper and bronze, and did not even have valves to use. The earliest brass instruments are trumpets and horns. The two era's that mostly revolutionized brass instruments were the renaissaince era and 19th century. The renaissaince era saw home to the evolution of this category of instruments before our eyes with instrument makers developing and advancing these instruments more and more such as the introduction of the slide trombone. 19th century Brass instruments saw the rise of valved trumpets, the french horn, and baritones become so popular due to how versatile they were, and with the popularization of Jazz. Brass instruments in orchestra are usually the star of the show as they are loud and expressive. This site will be for anyone to look over the main brass instruments which consists of:

  • Trumpet
  • French Horn
  • Trombone
  • Tuba
