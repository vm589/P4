Brass instruments have been around for as long as ancient history has started to be recorded. It is believed that the trombone was invented in the 15th Century. In the early years, it was used only in religious ceremonies and therefore considered a "holy" instrument. It started to become more widely used in the 18th century after Beethoven used it in a symphony.
