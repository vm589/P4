Trumpets have had many Variations throughout history. Lets look through a few!


A modern day trumpet Bb Trumpet. Three valves made out of Brass
Most trumpets were used for military signalling as discussed earlier. You can play all the harmonic notes but these have been for the most part faded as modern bugles come with valves.
The Cornet can come in Bb or C variations. The difference between the two is that the cornet is used to play more melodic/mellower sound due to it being more compact. The Cornet and Trumpet have the same fingerings and sound amazing together
This is the first official variation of the instrument dating back to the 16th century. It was the first valveless iteration of the trumpet and the first iteration of the trumpet.
